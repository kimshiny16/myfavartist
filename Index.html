Hello, We are BTS

A self produced album: BE, Releasing November 20, 2020

We are a K-pop (or Korean pop) group made up of seven members - Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V and Jungkook - who come from all over South Korea.
We launched in 2013 and have since rocketed to global stardom.

Over the years, we have produced material around various themes starting with the 'School' trilogy. This was followed by the 'Most Beautiful Moment in Life', 'Wings', and most recently, the 'Love Yourself' series.

We aim to make people feel loved and precious through our music. When it comes to music, we never considered language as a barrier.
